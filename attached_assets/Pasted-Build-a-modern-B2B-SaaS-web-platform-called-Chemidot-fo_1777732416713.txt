Build a modern B2B SaaS web platform called “Chemidot” for the chemical and industrial materials industry.

Chemidot is a digital B2B marketplace that connects chemical buyers, suppliers, manufacturers, and distributors across the Middle East, especially Saudi Arabia. The platform enables companies to source chemicals, compare suppliers, request quotations, place orders, and participate in collective bulk purchasing to reduce costs and optimize logistics.

The platform should feel premium, enterprise-grade, investor-ready, and highly polished.

IMPORTANT:
The UI/UX inspiration should be similar to the modern industrial marketplace experience of Knowde, especially:

* Product detail page structure
* Marketplace browsing experience
* Supplier presentation
* Search experience
* Modern enterprise design language

Reference inspirations:

* Product page:
  [https://www.knowde.com/stores/braskem-america/products/braskem-america-gwax-50w](https://www.knowde.com/stores/braskem-america/products/braskem-america-gwax-50w)

* Marketplace page:
  [https://www.knowde.com/marketplace](https://www.knowde.com/marketplace)

DO NOT clone or copy the design exactly.
Instead:

* Create an original experience inspired by the same modern B2B marketplace style
* Use unique layouts, spacing, interactions, branding, and colors
* Simplify unnecessary complexity
* Improve usability where possible
* Make Chemidot feel like its own premium startup
